About FoodBridge

We're on a mission to reduce food waste and fight hunger by connecting food donors with organizations that help those in need.

Our Mission

Creating a Sustainable Food System

FoodShare was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to create a bridge between surplus food and those who need it most. We believe that no good food should go to waste while people go hungry.

  • Reduce Food Waste

    We help redirect surplus food that would otherwise be thrown away.

  • Fight Hunger

    We connect food donors with NGOs that distribute food to vulnerable populations.

  • Build Community

    We foster relationships between businesses, NGOs, and individuals to create lasting impact.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do as we work toward our mission.

Compassion

We believe in treating everyone with dignity and respect. Our work is driven by empathy for those facing food insecurity.

Efficiency

We strive to create the most efficient systems possible to ensure food reaches those in need quickly and safely.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing the environmental impact of food waste and promoting sustainable practices.
